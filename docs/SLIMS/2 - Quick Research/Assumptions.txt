ASSUMPTION #1: Students and supervisors have reliable internet access at training sites to use the system consistently
Risk: High
How to test: Survey students during pilot session about internet availability at placement organizations; check connectivity patterns in usage logs; interview industry supervisors about workplace internet policies
Status: Validated

ASSUMPTION #2: Industry supervisors will adopt magic link authentication and provide weekly feedback via email without needing passwords
Risk: High
How to test: Run pilot with 10-20 industry supervisors; track magic link click rates and comment completion rates; interview supervisors about login experience vs paper signatures
Status: Validated

---

ASSUMPTION #3: Students prefer digital logbook over traditional paper-based system despite being accustomed to handwritten entries
Risk: High
How to test: Survey students after pilot session comparing effort/convenience; measure completion rates of digital entries vs historical paper logbook submission rates; conduct focus group on user experience
Status: Validated
