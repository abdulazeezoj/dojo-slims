# SLIMS

**SIWES Logbook & Internship Management System**

A web-based platform that digitizes the entire SIWES supervision process at Ahmadu Bello University, replacing paper logbooks with a streamlined, transparent, and verifiable digital workflow.

## Overview

SLIMS eliminates the manual burden of maintaining handwritten logbooks for 24 weeks of industrial training. It creates permanent, verifiable digital records for institutional compliance while enabling remote supervision and reducing administrative overhead.

## For Students
- Document daily training activities digitally
- Upload diagrams and attachments
- Auto-generate print-ready physical logbooks on demand

## For Supervisors
- Industry supervisors provide feedback via email links (no login needed)
- School supervisors monitor progress remotely
- All reviews timestamped and permanently recorded

## For ABU SIWES Unit
- Centralized digital repository of all student training activities
- Automated supervision workflows
- Reduced paperwork with maintained compliance
